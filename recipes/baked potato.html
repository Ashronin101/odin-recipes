Baked Potato

How to make?

Baked potato is a classic dish that involves a variety of ingredients centered around a potato. It makes the potato more flavourful and looks appealing.

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Bacon
  • Green onions
  1. Heat the oven to 450*C
  2. Prepare baking sheet
  3. Poke the potato with a fork
  4. Bake the potato for about 25 minutes
  5. Take the potato out and brush with butter
  6. Bake the potato again for about 20 minutes and continuously add 5 minutes until the potato is soft
  7. Prepare the potato by first slicing the top half
  8. Scoop out a portion of the potato and mix it with any ingredients you want, such as bacon, green onions, cheese, etc
